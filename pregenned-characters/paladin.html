Feat: War Caster

Advantage on CON saves to maintain concentration spells. Can perform somatic components with weapons/shield in hand. Can cast spells as opportunity attacks.

Class Features

Lay on Hands15 HP pool / Long Rest
Action: touch a creature to restore any number of HP from your pool (pool = paladin level × 5 = 15 HP). Or spend 5 HP from the pool to cure one disease or neutralise one poison. You can distribute the pool across multiple creatures.
New player tip: This is free HP — it doesn't use a spell slot. Use it between fights to top up allies, or as an emergency action to stop someone from dying.
Divine Sense3 / Long Rest
Action: until the end of your next turn, you know the location of any celestial, fiend, or undead within 60 ft that isn't behind total cover. You also know if a place or object has been consecrated or desecrated.
Fighting Style: Protection
Reaction: when a creature you can see attacks a target other than you that is within 5 ft of you, you can impose disadvantage on the attack roll. Requires a shield. Excellent for guarding squishy allies.
Divine Smite
When you hit with a melee weapon, you can expend a spell slot to deal bonus radiant damage: 2d8 for a 1st-level slot. You decide after you know the hit lands — even after seeing the damage roll.
New player tip: Save smites for the boss (you only have 3 slots), and use them on confirmed hits against undead or fiends for the extra d8. A 1st-level smite on a crit = 4d8 radiant!
Channel Divinity1 / Short or Long Rest
Sacred Weapon: Action; for 1 minute, add CHA mod (+2) to attack rolls with one weapon, and it sheds bright light in 20 ft. Turn the Unholy: Undead and fiends within 30 ft must make a WIS save (DC 12) or flee for 1 minute. Either option, 1/rest.
Divine Health
You are immune to disease.

Oath of Devotion Spells (always prepared)

  • 1stSanctuary — Bonus action; target creature is warded: attackers must make WIS save DC 12 or choose a different target. Ends if the warded creature attacks or casts a harmful spell (1 min).
Prepared Spells (choose 3)
  • 1stBless — Concentration; up to 3 creatures add 1d4 to attack rolls and saves for 1 min. Arguably the best 1st-level spell in the game.
  • 1stCure Wounds — Touch; 1d8+2 HP healed.
  • 1stThunderous Smite — Concentration; next weapon hit deals +2d6 thunder and target must make STR save DC 12 or be knocked prone.
Tip: Bless is usually your round 1 cast. It stacks beautifully with your allies' rolls and is dramatically more valuable over a long fight than a single Smite.
Once Per Long Rest · Character Quirk
Inspiring Speech
You take one minute to deliver a short, sincere, and unambiguously heartfelt speech. Each willing creature that listens (up to 5) gains 1d4 temporary hit points. These do not stack. The speech can be about anything — your god, the cause, someone's personal bravery, the importance of breakfast. You must appear to genuinely mean it. Iona always genuinely means it. She has never once been insincere and considers this a point of both pride and minor social inconvenience.

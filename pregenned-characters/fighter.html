Feat: Tough

+2 HP per level = +6 HP at level 3. This pushes total HP to 34, making Cade significantly harder to drop than other frontliners.

Class Features

Fighting Style: Defense
+1 AC while wearing armour. Combined with chain mail + shield, this gives AC 19 — the highest achievable at level 3 without magic.
Second Wind1 / Short or Long Rest
Bonus action: regain 1d10+3 HP. Simple self-heal that doesn't need anyone's help. Use it whenever you're below half HP — there's no reason to save it when a short rest is the refresh.
New player tip: This is your personal insurance policy. You have it back after a short rest (10 minutes), so don't feel like you're "wasting" it.
Action Surge1 / Short or Long Rest
Once per short rest, on your turn you may take one additional action. This lets you attack twice in one turn (two attack actions), or attack + dash, or attack + shove, etc. The single most powerful move available to you.
New player tip: Save Action Surge for the boss or a critical moment when you need to drop an enemy before they act. Using both attack actions gives you two rolls each at +5.
Improved CriticalChampion
Your weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20 (instead of just 20). Critical hits double all damage dice. With a longsword, a crit deals 2d8+3 instead of 1d8+3.
Extra Attack
Not yet — this arrives at level 5 and grants two attacks per Attack action. At level 3 you make one attack per action (but Action Surge gives you a second action).
Once Per Long Rest · Character Quirk
Not Today
When you are reduced to 0 hit points, you can choose to drop to 1 hit point instead. You do not fall unconscious. You must, however, immediately shout one sentence out loud at the table — addressed to your attacker. The sentence must be defiant. The DM decides if it's good enough. (It always is. The rule about the DM deciding exists to make you commit to the moment.) Then keep fighting.

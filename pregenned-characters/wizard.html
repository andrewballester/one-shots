Class Features

Spellbook
Your spellbook contains 10 spells (6 at level 1 + 2 per level after). You can add more by copying found spells. Spells in the book aren't automatically available — you prepare 6 per day from it (INT mod + wizard level).
Arcane Recovery1 / Long Rest
Once per day, during a short rest, recover spent spell slots with combined level equal to or less than half your wizard level (rounded up = 2). So: one 2nd-level slot, or two 1st-level slots. Doesn't work on slots above 5th level.
New player tip: Short rest after the first fight and immediately recover two 1st-level slots. This effectively gives you 6 first-level slots per day instead of 4.
Evocation SavantSchool of Evocation
The gold and time you must spend to copy an evocation spell into your spellbook is halved. Not relevant mid-session but important for between sessions.
Sculpt SpellsSchool of Evocation
When you cast an evocation spell that forces a saving throw, you can choose up to (1 + the spell's level) creatures that automatically succeed on the save AND take no damage from the spell — even on a failed save for everyone else.
New player tip: This is why Evocation wizards can drop Fireball or Burning Hands into a crowd of enemies and allies without hurting the allies. At level 3: cast Burning Hands (1st level) and protect up to 2 allies inside the cone. Drop Shatter (2nd level), protect up to 3. You are now a safe AoE blaster.
Once Per Long Rest · Character Quirk
Excessive Footnote
You recall — at length, and out loud — a highly specific piece of magical lore that happens to be relevant to the current situation. The information is always technically accurate and usually more detailed than anyone asked for. Mechanically: one creature (you or an ally) gains advantage on one Arcana, History, or Investigation check related to a magical effect or creature. You must deliver at least two sentences of lore to activate this. If you enjoy it more than they do, it still works.

Cantrips (no slot needed)
  • CantripFire Bolt — +5, 2d10 fire, 120 ft. Primary ranged attack.
  • CantripRay of Frost — +5, 1d8 cold, 60 ft; target speed −10 ft until your next turn. Use to let allies kite enemies.
  • CantripMage Hand — Spectral hand within 30 ft; manipulate objects. Utility king.
  • CantripPrestidigitation — Minor magical tricks. Indispensable out of combat.
Spellbook (10 spells known) — Prepare 6 per Long Rest
  • 1stMage Armor — Touch self; AC = 13+DEX for 8 hrs. Cast every morning. No concentration.
  • 1stMagic Missile — 3 darts auto-hit; 1d4+1 force each. Use when you absolutely cannot afford a miss.
  • 1stBurning Hands — DEX save DC 13; 3d6 fire in 15 ft cone. Sculpt Spells lets you exclude allies.
  • 1stThunderwave — CON save DC 13; 2d8 thunder in 15 ft cube; push 10 ft. Good panic-button escape.
  • 1stShield — Reaction; +5 AC until your next turn, including vs the triggering attack. Best defensive spell at this level.
  • 1stDetect Magic — Ritual; sense magic within 30 ft and know the school; concentration, 10 min. Free with ritual (no slot).
  • 2ndScorching Ray — +5; three rays of 2d6 fire; split among targets. Best DPR at this level.
  • 2ndShatter — CON save DC 13; 3d8 thunder in 10 ft sphere. Destroys objects; bad for constructs.
  • 2ndMisty Step — Bonus action; teleport 30 ft to visible space. Your emergency escape hatch.
  • 2ndWeb — DEX save DC 13; 20 ft cube of webbing; creatures inside restrained; concentration, 1 hr. Best area control at level 3.
Tip: Default prepared set: Mage Armor (auto), Magic Missile, Burning Hands, Scorching Ray, Shatter, Web. Sculpt Spells + Burning Hands in melee = safe AoE. Web + allies with AoO = trap room. Shield on reaction = don't get hit.
